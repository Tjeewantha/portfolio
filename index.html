About

I am an aspiring IT professional with a strong passion for networking and cybersecurity. While I have a keen interest in both fields, I've recently focused on sharpening my networking skills to build a solid foundation for a future in IT infrastructure and security.

The Story

I hold an Advanced Higher National Diploma (AHND) in Cybersecurity and am currently pursuing a BSc in Cybersecurity and Networking Technology. Throughout my academic journey, I’ve completed several solo projects related to both network configuration and cyber defense, which have helped me apply my knowledge in practical, real-world scenarios. Although I haven’t held a formal IT role yet, I’ve actively contributed to solving POS system issues and connectivity problems at the hotel where I work part-time as a cashier. These hands-on experiences, though informal, have given me valuable insights into troubleshooting and maintaining IT systems in real-world environments. To continuously sharpen my cybersecurity skills, I regularly engage in CTF (Capture The Flag) challenges, a hobby that started during my diploma studies and has become a vital part of my learning journey.
